RE:  Introducing the Busoga Ghetto Structure, a system to profile and monitor ghetto activities

For the past two months, I have been quietly building a system for you, a tool that lets you monitor ghetto activities from the comfort of your office and your home. It is an application that sits on your phone and on the office computer, so the entire structure is always in the palm of your hand.

At its heart, the system profiles the ghetto. Every member is profiled with their photo, National Identification Number, location and depot, and is then issued a unique serial number and a QR code that anyone can scan to verify them on the spot.

What the system does
  • Profiles every ghetto beneficiary across all 12 districts of Busoga, organised by district and by depot.
  • Captures the full profile: photo, NIN, sub-county, parish, village, depot and depot leadership role.
  • Tracks disbursements, the SACCO loan advanced at 6% interest, repayable within twelve (12) months.
  • Tracks repayments, outstanding balances and overdue loans, district by district.
  • Generates clean reports per district and per depot, ready to print or export.
  • Issues each beneficiary an export card carrying a working QR code for instant verification.
  • Keeps an audit trail of who did what, and when.
It carries roles, so the right people see the right things
  • Administrator: yourself, with a full view of everything across all 12 districts.
  • RDC: oversees activities within their own district.
  • Profiler: registers and profiles members in the field.

I would like to request an appointment to fully present the system to you when you come to Jinja, so you can see it working from end to end. I have also attached a small presentation to usher you into what the system is capable of.
